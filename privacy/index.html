Privacy Policy

Last updated: 11 May 2026

This page explains what personal data Arxyne Ltd collects when you visit arxyne.com, what we do with it, and the rights you have under UK data protection law.

Who we are

Controller: Arxyne Ltd

Jurisdiction: England & Wales (Companies House registered)

Contact: chris@arxyne.com

Arxyne Ltd is the data controller for personal data collected through this website. We do not have a separate Data Protection Officer; for any data-protection enquiry, write to the contact address above.

What we collect

We collect the minimum needed to keep the site working and to understand which pages are useful. Specifically:

  • Analytics data (only with your consent) — pages viewed, approximate location (country-level), browser type, time on page, referring site. Collected through Google Analytics 4 (property G-L2KSD72508) and only after you click Accept analytics on the consent banner. IP addresses are anonymised by GA4 before storage.
  • Server logs — our hosting provider (GitHub Pages) retains short-lived request logs containing IP address, user agent and URL for security and abuse prevention. We do not access or analyse these logs.
  • Email correspondence — if you email us (chris@arxyne.com), we keep the message so we can reply and follow up.

We do not use marketing trackers, advertising pixels, session replay, or fingerprinting. We do not sell personal data.

Legal basis

  • Analytics — your explicit consent (UK GDPR Art. 6(1)(a) and PECR Reg. 6). You can withdraw consent at any time via the Cookie settings link in the footer; that immediately prevents further analytics loading.
  • Server logs — our legitimate interests in keeping the site secure and available (UK GDPR Art. 6(1)(f)).
  • Email correspondence — our legitimate interests in responding to you, or the steps you ask us to take before entering into an agreement (UK GDPR Art. 6(1)(b)/(f)).

Cookies

The site sets no cookies until you choose. If you click Accept analytics, Google Analytics sets first-party cookies (_ga, _ga_*) on the .arxyne.com domain to distinguish unique visitors. Full details, lifetimes, and how to clear them are on the cookie policy.

Where your data is processed

Analytics data is processed by Google LLC (United States) acting as our processor. Google transfers are covered by the EU–US and UK Data Privacy Framework. Server logs sit with GitHub, Inc. (United States), also under Data Privacy Framework. We process emails on UK-based infrastructure.

How long we keep it

  • Analytics data — 14 months (GA4 default).
  • Server logs — retained by the hosting provider per their policy (typically days to weeks).
  • Email correspondence — kept as long as there is a live conversation or business relationship, then archived for up to 6 years for accounting and legal reasons.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR you have the usual data-subject rights of access, rectification, erasure, restriction, objection, withdrawal of consent, and portability. You also have the right to complain to the UK Information Commissioner's Office at ico.org.uk. The contact address for any of the above is in the Who we are section above.

Children

arxyne.com is aimed at engineers and technical buyers. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe we have, email us and we will delete it.

Changes

If we change this policy materially, we will update the date above and, where appropriate, surface a notice on the site. The current version always lives at arxyne.com/privacy/.
